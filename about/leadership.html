ABOUT US / LEADERSHIP TEAM

A Team That Has Built This Before. Together.

Over 100 years of combined experience building and scaling innovation-driven technology businesses in the US and India.

FlywheelTech is led by a team of experienced operators who have worked together before — not a group assembled around a thesis, but a founding team with a shared track record and a shared conviction about what mid-size BFSI companies need.

Vikram Malaney

Director

B.S. Computer Engineering, Duke University

Vikram brings over 30 years of experience managing private, public, and startup businesses. He began his career in software development and product management at a Silicon Valley startup pioneering speech synthesis technologies. He subsequently co-founded and managed the India GCC innovation center of the AI mortgage processing platform that processed 30% of US home loans, growing it to over 2,000 employees. As Director of the Vulcan Group, Vikram now oversees the overall operations of FlywheelTech.

Rajesh Shah

Head of Operations

46 years of operations experience

Rajesh has handled three startups in his career and brings over 46 years of experience managing operations and corporate services including legal, finance, and operations. He played a key role in growing and managing the India GCC for the same mortgage AI platform to over 2,000 team members across 200,000 square feet. He currently heads operations and corporate services for FlywheelTech in India.

Suvir Malaney

Head, Europe

BE Industrial Engineering, NCSU; MBA, Thunderbird (ASU)

Suvir brings extensive experience managing businesses across the US, India, and Europe. Based in France, he manages FlywheelTech's European operations — building and managing relationships with companies across the EU and UK interested in establishing AI-focused GCCs.

Muralidhar Thyagarajan

Head, North America

BE Mechanical, IIT Kharagpur; MBA, XLRI

With over 20 years of experience in mortgage and financial services across the US and India, Muralidhar manages FlywheelTech's North America operations from Silicon Valley — building and managing client relationships with BFSI companies pursuing AI GCC strategies.

Davies Thalakottur

Head, India Technology

Technology Operations

Davies brings extensive experience building and managing teams and architecting solutions across storage, proptech, traveltech, and fintech. He manages FlywheelTech's India technology operations — including hiring and training development teams and managing IT facilities and infrastructure.

RGG Desai

Head, Finance & Regulatory

Finance & Regulatory Operations

Desai brings extensive experience in finance, accounts, risk, and regulatory management across public companies and startups. He served as Director and CFO of the India GCC for the mortgage AI platform, handling the merger with Ellie Mae and the subsequent sale to ICE Mortgage Technology. He currently heads finance, accounts, and regulatory operations for FlywheelTech.

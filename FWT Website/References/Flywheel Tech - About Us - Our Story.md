
FlywheelTech was founded on a simple premise: That the current technology and business models driving the AI revolution are not appropriate or financially viable for Mid-Size business who can neither afford the costs at scale or risk of managing “Black Box” AI Models.  The world's largest financial institutions have spent decades building solutions inside their own Global Capability Centers which should also be available to growth-stage BFSI and Fintech companies — without the dependency, the knowledge leakage, or the vendor capture that comes with renting it.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Leveraging its experience, the Flywheel Tech founding team spent over a decade building an AI-integrated mortgage platform for US regulated financial services — growing from a two-person India team to over 1,400 employees, processing 15% of US home loans, and serving 6 of the top 10 US mortgage originators and servicers. Every architecture decision was made under live regulatory scrutiny, and every model decision had to be explainable to an auditor.

What they learned in that journey — how to hire right in India, how to build an “Innovation” culture across a 9.5-hour time zone gap, how to structure an AI-capable team that delivers production-grade accuracy, and how to design an organization that its parent company can genuinely own and operate — is not something you can find in a consulting report. It is operational knowledge, earned and learned the hard way, over time.

FlywheelTech was created to make that knowledge available to the next generation of BFSI and Fintech companies navigating the same challenge: they need to build AI capability they own, they cannot afford to build it entirely onshore, and they do not have the operational playbook for building it in India — or in the nearshore locations that may suit their strategy better.

We have that playbook. And we transfer these key capabilities in a planned and effective manner to our clients.

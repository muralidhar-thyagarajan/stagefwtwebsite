## Page 6 — Services

**Narrative role:** Resolution mechanics — how the engagement actually works  
**Governing question:** *What am I actually buying, and how does it begin?*  
**Emotional job:** Clarify → De-risk

### Consulting — *The entry point*
Before you commit, FWT helps you decide. Pre-GCC advisory through to post-transfer architecture. Three phases: pre-creation, during build, post-transfer. No dependency on proceeding to BOT.

### Build-Operate-Transfer — *The core engagement*
Three SKUs: Launchpad, Growth, Stable — defined by activity and outcome, not time. Managed infrastructure and corporate services continue beyond transfer (graphic required here).

### Managed Services — *The long-term operating layer*
Co-working incubation through to managed workspace. Clarify: managed services persist after BOT transfer — not a transitional offering. Does not position GCC as fully flexible/scalable.

### Decision Operations (Human-in-Loop) — *The AI-enabled operating model in practice*
Theme: *AI-enabled, human-powered.* The operational layer where oversight, orchestration, and governance live. Maps directly to the 10-domain operating model introduced in Page 2.

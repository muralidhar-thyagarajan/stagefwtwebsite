OUR TRACK RECORD

This Is Not a Case Study. This Is What We Are.

The FlywheelTech founding team designed, built, scaled, and exited an AI-integrated platform in regulated financial services.

They started with two people in India and a mandate to build production AI for US mortgage lending. They ended with over 2,000 employees, serving 150+ US customers — including six of the top ten US mortgage originators and servicers — processing 30% of US home loans. Every model decision was made under live regulatory scrutiny. Every architecture choice had to be explainable to an auditor. Hallucinations were not a product quality issue — they were a compliance liability. The institutional knowledge accumulated in that GCC was the asset that made the company acquirable. That knowledge is what FlywheelTech offers.

HOW IT WORKS

Build. Operate. Transfer. Then Step Away.

BOT is now the mainstream mid-market entry path. Our entire business is designed around doing it correctly.

The BOT model has grown from under 10% of new GCC setups a few years ago to approximately 40% today. You engage a partner who has the operational playbook, let them build and operate through to maturity, and take full ownership when the capability is ready to run independently.

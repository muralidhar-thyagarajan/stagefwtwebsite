THE NUMBERS

The AI Talent Advantage Is Real. The Numbers Are Not Close.

120,000 AI professionals already embedded in Indian GCCs. 40–60% cost advantage over US or European equivalents.

India's GCC ecosystem reached $69.85 billion in 2025 and is projected to exceed $130 billion by 2033. Over 1,700 Global Capability Centers employ 1.9 million professionals. Pune — where FlywheelTech operates — has grown from 210 GCCs in 2019 to over 360 in 2025, the fastest-growing GCC city in India, concentrated specifically in BFSI, fintech, and engineering. GenAI and LLM hiring demand grew 300% year-over-year. The talent is not coming — it is already there, working in production AI environments. The cost advantage — 40–60% versus US or European equivalents — is structural, not cyclical.

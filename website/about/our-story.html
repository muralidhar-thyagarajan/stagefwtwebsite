ABOUT US / OUR STORY

WHY WE EXIST

The GCC Model That Works for the Largest Banks Should Also Work for the Next Generation of BFSI.

FlywheelTech was founded to make that possible — without the vendor dependency, the knowledge leakage, or the capture that comes with renting it.

The world's largest financial institutions — JPMorgan, HSBC, Goldman Sachs — have built AI capability inside their own Global Capability Centers over decades. These GCCs are not outsourcing arrangements. They are permanent, captive capability centers where institutional knowledge accumulates, IP is owned, and the organisation builds genuine AI advantage over time. Mid-size BFSI companies and fintech founders have never had access to that model at a scale that works for them. That is the gap FlywheelTech was founded to close.

WHAT WE BUILT

We Grew from Two People to Over 1,400. In a Regulated Industry. Across a 9.5-Hour Time Zone Gap.

Everything FlywheelTech offers was learned by doing it — under real operational pressure, at real scale, under live regulatory scrutiny.

FlywheelTech's founding team spent over a decade building an AI-integrated software platform for US regulated financial services. They started with two people in India and a mandate. They ended with over 1,400 employees, processing 15% of US home loans, and serving six of the top ten US mortgage originators and servicers.

What they learned in that journey — how to hire right in India, how to build an innovation culture across a time zone gap, how to structure an AI-capable team that delivers production-grade accuracy, how to make AI explainable to a regulator, and how to design an organisation that its parent company can genuinely own and operate — is not something you can find in a consulting report.

It is operational knowledge. Earned and learned the hard way. Over time. Under conditions that forced them to get it right.

WHY FLYWHEELTECH

We Have That Playbook. And We Transfer It.

FlywheelTech was not created to consult on GCCs. It was created to build them — and hand them over.

When the founding team exited the company they had spent a decade building, they looked at the landscape of mid-size BFSI companies and fintech founders facing the same challenge they had just solved — and found no partner who could offer what they had been: an operationally credible, end-to-end GCC builder with genuine BFSI AI experience, a methodology forged in production, and a business model designed to produce client independence.

FlywheelTech was founded to be that partner. Not a consultancy that advises on GCCs. Not a recruitment firm that provides GCC headcount. A firm that builds AI GCCs — correctly, for BFSI, end-to-end — and transfers them as functioning, permanently owned capability centers.

FlywheelTech is backed by the Vulcan Group, whose 50+ years of building and scaling innovation-driven companies in the US and India provides operational infrastructure, regulatory relationships, and deep local expertise that typical GCC consultants cannot access.

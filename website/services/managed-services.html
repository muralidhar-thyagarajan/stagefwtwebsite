SERVICES / MANAGED SERVICES

MANAGED SERVICES

Your GCC Runs. The Infrastructure Behind It Keeps Running Too.

Managed infrastructure and corporate services that extend well beyond the BOT timeline — so you focus on building AI capability, not running a legal entity.

Building a GCC requires an operational infrastructure that most mid-size BFSI companies are not equipped to run directly from their home market. Employment law compliance, statutory and regulatory filings, finance and accounting operations, facilities management, IT infrastructure, and payroll — these are not trivial to operate in a new jurisdiction, and they are not the reason you are building a GCC.

FlywheelTech manages this infrastructure layer so that your team can focus on what it is actually there to do: build AI capability.

THE SERVICE

From Co-Working Incubation to Full Managed Workspace — the Infrastructure Your GCC Needs.

Two stages of infrastructure, matched to the maturity of your GCC.

Co-working incubation

In the early stages of the BOT engagement, your team operates from co-working space — ready to use immediately, no infrastructure build required. This allows the GCC to become operational in weeks rather than months, while the permanent workspace is established.

Managed workspace

As the GCC grows, it transitions to a dedicated, permanently staffed managed workspace with full facilities management, IT infrastructure, and operational support. This is the long-term operating environment for a mature GCC.

Across both stages, FlywheelTech manages:

  • • Legal entity compliance and statutory filings
  • • Finance and accounting operations
  • • Payroll and HR administration
  • • IT infrastructure and support
  • • Facilities management
  • • Vendor management for operational suppliers

IMPORTANT CLARIFICATION

Managed Services Is a Division of Labour. Not a Path to Dependency.

You own the capability, the people, and the IP. We run the infrastructure that lets them operate effectively.

Managed Services is sometimes confused with the kind of managed services that creates long-term vendor dependency — where the vendor controls the capability and the client remains reliant on them to deliver it.

FlywheelTech's Managed Services is the opposite. It is operational infrastructure management — the things that do not differentiate your business but must be done well for your business to operate. Your team's work, your models, your institutional knowledge — these are entirely yours. We manage the compliance filings, the office, the IT, and the payroll.

The GCC is not positioned as a flexible, scalable workforce. It is a permanent, dedicated capability center. Managed Services is the infrastructure layer that allows it to function as one.
